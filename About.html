About my kitchen

B.B's kitchen is a wonderful and very cool place where people can enjoy a delicious traditional Hausa vegetarian meals,


and also is a place where we teaching you a selection of rice and Tuwo dishes.

and how to create the most delicious sauces for use in a range of Hausa cookery.

We also offer birthday cake, cupcake icing and decorating stations to delight and entertain your guests

also we show you how to create beautifully designed cakes and cupcakes.
